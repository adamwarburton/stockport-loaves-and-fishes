Right, summer's packing up. Time for us to do the opposite.

Every autumn the same thing happens: the temperature drops, and the queue for coats gets longer than the queue for seconds. So we're stocking up early this year, and you can help:

- **Coats, hats, gloves and scarves** — winter-weight
- **Sleeping bags and blankets**
- **Thermal anything**
- **Flasks** — a hot drink that stays hot is worth two that don't

Everything you bring goes straight from your hands to someone who'll be wearing it that same evening. No warehouse, no sorting depot — just a table on Thomson Street and people who need what's on it.

Drop-offs every Sunday, 5:00–6:30pm, at Stockport Baptist Church, SK3 9DR.
