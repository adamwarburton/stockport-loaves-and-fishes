A quick one, because the washing-up won't do itself.

To everyone who dropped off coats, tins and sleeping bags this month — thank you. To the people who pressed a note into a volunteer's hand and wouldn't take no for an answer — thank you. To whoever keeps donating the good biscuits — we know what you're doing, and it's working.

Every bit of it went where you'd hope: onto plates, onto backs, and into backpacks on Thomson Street on a Sunday evening.

Small charity. Big pan. Very grateful.
