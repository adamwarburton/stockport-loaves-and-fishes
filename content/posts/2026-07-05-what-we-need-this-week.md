The pans are on, the kettle's never off — but the cupboard could use a top-up. If you're having a clear-out or doing a big shop this week, here's what would help most:

- **Men's winter coats** — all sizes, large especially
- **Boots and trainers, size 9 and up** — worn is fine, holes are not
- **Sleeping bags** — clean and dry, please
- **Ring-pull tins** — stew, curry, meatballs, anything that makes a dinner

Bring anything on the list to Stockport Baptist Church, Thomson Street, SK3 9DR on Sunday between 5:00 and 6:30pm. A volunteer will take it off your hands and say thank you at least twice.

Can't get to us on a Sunday? [Get in touch](/contact) and we'll figure something out.
