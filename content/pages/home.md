title: "Welcome"
---
Every Sunday evening, in a church hall on Thomson Street, a bunch of volunteers put on a hot dinner for anyone in Stockport who needs one. Some of our guests are sleeping rough. Some are sofa-surfing. Some just can't stretch the money to the end of the week. All of them are welcome — and so are you.

No referral. No form. No sermon required. Just come. We've saved you a seat.
