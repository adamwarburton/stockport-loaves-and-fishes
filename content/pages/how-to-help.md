## Volunteer

Can you stir a pot, carry a table, or hold a conversation? You're hired. (We don't pay. But the company's excellent.)

We always need Sunday-evening hands — cooking, serving, setting up, packing down, and sitting with our guests over dinner, which is secretly the most important job of the lot. You don't need experience and you don't need to come every week.

[Email us](mailto:info@stockportloavesandfishes.org?subject=I%27d%20like%20to%20volunteer) or ring 07429 567402 and we'll find you an apron.

## Spread the word

This one's free. Tell someone about us — especially someone who might need a hot dinner on a Sunday. Stick our name in your church newsletter, your group chat, your works canteen.

And if you know someone sleeping rough in Stockport, tell them where we are: Thomson Street, Sundays, 5pm. That sentence has filled more seats than anything else we've ever done.
